Rivendell

Rivendell (Imladris in Elvish) is an elven settlement located in the north of Middle-earth, at the foothills of the Misty Mountains. Founded by Elrond in the Second Age, this valley serves as both a sanctuary and a house of wisdom. The Fellowship of the Ring was formed here in The Lord of the Rings. Rivendell is famous for its healing powers, libraries, and works of art. Its architecture is designed in harmony with nature, surrounded by waterfalls and gardens.
