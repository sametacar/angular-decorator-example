Mordor

Mordor is a dark land located in the southeast of Middle-earth, ruled by the Dark Lord Sauron. Surrounded by mountains on three sides, this land has a volcanic terrain. The active volcano called Orodruin (Mount Doom) is located here, and the One Ring was forged in the fires of this mountain.
